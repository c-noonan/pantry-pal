Pantry Pal

Unused food and leftovers are a problem everyone faces. Ever find yourself unsure of what you can make with that half full bottle of teriyaki sauce? $165 billion worth of food is wasted each year by Americans. Pantry Pal solves this problem by searching a recipe database and determining which recipes are readily available to make. Users can customize their list of current ingredients and find new or favorite recipes to make. Sign up and start cooking today!
